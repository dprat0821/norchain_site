PROV Service & PROV DEVELOPER

PROV stands for Provision, Representation, Orientation and Verification - Four essential phases abstracted from any service instances in IoT sharing economy.
Conventional IoT service model has three key roles: manufacturer, provider and consumer. Leverage the power of blockchain, we introduce a new independent role PROV Developer, who helps bridging provider and consumer with highly flexible and customizable services, and earns royalty when any instance of those services finalized.
NEOT project aims to build a PROV compatiable blockchain platform. PROV developers can retrieve the public information (service success rate, times and quality of service, payment history, etc.) from the blockchain to optimize their services, and use NEOT token to realise instant payment.
NEOT keeps large data transfers off-chain, and utilizes distributed ledger's great features of irreversibility, privacy-preservation and security. Differ from many other IoT distributed ledger projects, NEOT also targets to provide tools for PROV developers to implement "Proof-of-Service" in PROV's Verification phase, automate the service verification and reviewing process in many common user cases, without human authority's influence.
For more information about how does PROV service works, please read norchain's Yellow Paper.
