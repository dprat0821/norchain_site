NEO of Things

 The 1st practical distributed ledger IOT Project

NEOT = (NEO + PROV) * IoT

Comparing with other IoT distributed ledger projects' foundations, NEO has the advantages as a DAPP platform with high transaction speed and reasonable incentive mode. The support of light nodes and dBFT consensus are also suitable for the capacity limited IoT devices. Moreover, NEO's binary numeral system is compatible with most manufacturers' devices, avoiding unnecessary computational overhead.
Those are the reasons why NEO is the perfect block infrastructure for NEOT. PROV's service model with NEO's blockchain could form the best practical IoT solution.

PROV Service & PROV DEVELOPER

PROV stands for Provision, Representation, Orientation and Verification - Four essential phases abstracted from any service instances in IoT sharing economy.
Conventional IoT service model has three key roles: manufacturer, provider and consumer. Leverage the power of blockchain, we introduce a new independent role PROV Developer, who helps bridging provider and consumer with highly flexible and customizable services, and earns royalty or penalty when any instance of those services finalized.
NEOT project aims to build a PROV compatiable blockchain platform. PROV developers can retrieve the public information (service success rate, times and quality of service, payment history, etc.) from the blockchain to optimize their implementation of services, and use NEOT token to realise instant payment.
NEOT keeps large data transfers off-chain, and utilizes distributed ledger's great features of irreversibility, privacy-preservation and security. Differ from many other IoT distributed ledger projects, NEOT also targets to provide tools for PROV developers to implement "Proof-of-Service" in PROV's Verification phase, automate the service verification and reviewing process in many common user cases, without human authority's influence.
For more information about how does PROV service works, please read norchain's Yellow Paper.

USER CASE: SMart Transportation

PROV can make many interesting experience in consumer facing scenario. Meanwhile, Norchain also connects with enterprises and governments to realize the concept in inital stage. In the smart transportation scenario, no matter vehicles, repair shop or traffic camera, the PROV services process the data collected from the sensors, do the reflection immediately or transfer data from offchain channels, and then record the incidents onchain.
The researchers can retrieve the irreversible onchain record to optimize our traffic system. credit related services can ask users to prove their vechicle's onchain account to assess their behavior, in a very transparent manner and low maintainance cost. norchain is also proactively looking for cooperation from autonomous car and user-based insurance companies.

NEOT White Paper:
In this white paper, we first discuss the common challenges of distributed ledger based IoT projects, then introduce a new blockchain based service model PROV to help overcome some practical problems. The project NEOT (NEO of Things) is proposed to leverage PROV service model and NEO blockchain. In the last, we discuss some NEOT user cases in the smart traffic scenario.

PROV Yellow Paper(Beta):
In this yellow paper, we make a indepth discussion in technology perspective about an optimized blockchain share economy model: PROV. The role PROV developer as an independent party is introduced to decouple service providers from implementing services. PROV developer provides customers more flexibility in deciding how providers can serve them with their resources, and get profit from every instance of the service they created. By utilize blockchain infrastructure, PROV developers can retrieve useful information from the irreversible ledger to optimize their service.
